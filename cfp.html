Welcome to Learning by Asking for Intelligent Robots and Agents (LA4IRA@ROMAN’23)!

Topic of interest (including but not limited to)

We invite submission of papers describing innovative research and applications around the following topics. Papers that introduce new theoretical concepts or methods, help to develop a better understanding of new emerging concepts through extensive experiments, or demonstrate a novel application of these methods to a domain are encouraged.

  • Curiosity, Intentionality and Initiative in Interaction
  • Linguistic Communication and Dialogue
  • Learning and Knowledge Growth through Question and Answering
  • Multimodal Interaction and Conversational Skills
  • Multi-modal Situation Awareness and Spatial Cognition
  • Multi-modal Uncertainty Awareness
  • Robot Perception for Interaction and Communication such as Robot Audtion, Robot Vision, etc.
  • Embodied Learning (Embodiment, Empathy and Intersubjectivity)
  • Question answering for video understanding
  • Deep Learning archittecture for multi-modal video understanding representation

Paper submission

We welcome papers ranging between 1 - 2 pages of abstract, excluding all references and appendices. All submissions should be formatted in the IEEE 2023 format. A LaTeX template is available here. A Word template is available here. Following the IEEE RO-MAN conference submission policy, all papers are reviewed using a single-blind review process: authors declare their names and affiliations in the paper for the reviewers to see, but reviewers do not know each other's identities, nor do the authors receive information about who has reviewed their papers. Contents presented at other conferences can also be submitted again if the topic of paper is relevant to our workshop. The accepted papers will be posted on the workshop website and will NOT appear in the IEEE RO-MAN proceedings. Presenting papers at this workshop does not affect you to submit your work to other venues.
